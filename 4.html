How a phrase triggers complex logic:
Step A: Phrase
C-4 01 (Note triggers Inst 01)
Step B: Instrument

3. Table Tips
TIC Speed:
  • 01/02: Audio Rate (FM, Chords, Timbres)
  • 06: Standard Rhythm (Groove synced)
Interpolation:
Highlight Start & End row.
Press OPT + EDIT to auto-fill ramp.
